Industry Leaders

In the car industry, some companies are considered leaders because they are really good at making cars and coming up with new ideas. Toyota is known for reliable cars and popular hybrids like the Prius. Volkswagen makes a lot of different types of cars and is famous around the world. General Motors (GM) has been making cars for a long time with brands like Chevrolet and Cadillac. They are also working on electric and self-driving cars. Tesla, led by Elon Musk, is changing the game with their electric cars and cool technology. These companies are the ones pushing the industry forward and making cars that everyone talks about
